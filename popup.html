Unlock Natural Voices

Chrome supports high-quality Natural voices, but you must enable them once:

🔍 Click to enlarge
  1. Select some text on any article webpage.
  2. Right-click the text and choose Open in reading mode.
  3. In the Reading Mode side panel, click the Settings icon (gear).
  4. Click Voice selection ➔ Languages....
  5. Toggle ON the languages you want. Chrome will download the natural voices.
